What is USCO?

Common hardware platforms for IoT requires signal voltages to be much lower than those generated by industrial sensors and actuators. For this reason Universal Signal voltage level Converter (USCO) was designed. It can convert voltage levels of both analog and digital signals.

  • Learn More

USCO Design

  • Motherboard design

    The Motherboard is the core of USCO. It feeds digital and analog converting modules with required power and acts as a base for interactions between sensors and IoT hardware platforms.

  • Digital converter

    The Digital converter has two bidirectional channels allowing a usage of only one pin in each four sides of converter.

  • Analog converter

    The Analog converter has two unidirectional channels: one for signal converting from high voltage side to low voltage and another for vice versa convert.

Klick Technologies

Klick Technologies Ltd, is a private company emerged in November 2015 as a Spin-off from the Communications and Networking Department,School of Electrical Engineering of Aalto University, Finland.

Klick Technologies Ltd is aiming to commercialize the results and expertise acquired in free research by its founding members in the area of Internet of Things. The founders of the company have very long and extensive experience working for different large and small companies as well as in academia.

Klick Technologies Ltd delivers modular, easy-to-configure, easy-to-program, scalable, and open source Internet-of-Things platform. The platform enables system integrators and providers in different application domains to deploy their solutions with minimum time-to-market.
